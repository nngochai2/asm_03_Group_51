Today, the presence of VPNs is expanding, especially the presence of some giants such as ExpressVPN (a VPN company with more than 3000 servers in more than 90 countries, including several locations) and NordVPN (a VPN software with the highest level of security today). However, disadvantages still exist in those VPN software. Take, for example, in order to have a both private and most importantly, fast Internet connection on some VPN software, the users have to pay a specific amount of money to get that service. Therefore, our aim is not to compete with super secure VPN software above, but to develop user-friendly VPN software with simple design, high security and one hundred percent free.
