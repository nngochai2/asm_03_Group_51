1. Nguyen Ngoc Hai

“Hello! My name is Nguyen Ngoc Hai, I am 18 years old and currently I am a student at RMIT University in Hanoi, Vietnam. Personally, since I was just a little child, I have always been amazed by the way how a specific piece of technology works, such as an iPhone or an iPod. Gradually, my curiosity led to the passion in science in general. Hence, I always question myself that what was more reasonable than choosing my career path as Information Technology. From the first day I began my study in RMIT, I always satisfy with my decision since I can have a chance to learn what I love and what I want. I can finally apply the knowledge about Python and Command Prompt in specific tasks. In terms of my future career, I always find questions about that topic difficult to have an answer, since my passion might not meet the requirements of my future life and necessities. Nevertheless, if money did not matter, I would really love to work in Cyber Security department, because it is my belief that the more advanced that the technology gets, the more dangers users might face up with. Moreover, I normally enjoys reading newspaper so that I can always be aware of the problems relating to cyber security in this day and age and what people have done to prevent it.”
