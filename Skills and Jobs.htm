Our nececessary positions in the future

N.O Position Description
1 Full-stack developer Firstly, in terms of the visual, structure, system, data, and logic aspect, we would select full stack developers who can do well in both frontend and backend. In order to reduce stress and pressure, we will need two people with such skills.
2 Tester After having personnel in product development, tester is the position that we will look for, to make sure our software remains stable. To be able to enroll in this position, a participant must understand nowadays trend and preferences, so that he or she can advise the developers.
3 Marketing Department If the project is completed, the next thing that we have to do will be bringing the product to the market and the users. This department had to be able to introduce our product to a wide range of customers, so that the product can be well-known enough. Hence, this position requires support from people with good marketing skills.
4 Accountant The accountant is the member that we want to recruit. When the technical steps go smoothly, the economy will be the next problem that we have to consider and the accountant, who has good financial ability, will be the one to collect, process, check, analyze and provide economic information, economic and financial in the form of value, in kind and labor time.
