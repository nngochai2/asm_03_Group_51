1. How did we test the project?

We typically request that close family and friends visit our website first, have a look around, and provide feedback on how things are going as well as suggestions on how we can improve it. Moreover, we also created a survey on Google Forms, so that the users could mark our website. In detail, the survey consisted of the following questions:

  • Is the website easy to use?
  • Is the website eye-catching
  • Can it be used noramlly on mobile phone?
  • What can we do to improve your experience?
